You are in Westville, a part of Allshire.

Choices

Each round, you are given a coin and can choose to either:
  1. Invest in Allshire.
  2. Invest in Westville.
  3. Invest in Eastburgh
  4. Keep the coin.
The other players have the same decision.

Payoffs

Each round, bonus payments are earned depending on how people invest:
  1. If at least 4 players choose to invest in Allshire, then all 6 players get + {{ gameConstants.GLOBAL_REWARD }} coins.
  2. If at least 2 players choose to invest in Westville, then all 3 Westville players get + {{ gameConstants.LOCAL_REWARD }} coins.
  3. If at least 2 players choose to invest in Eastburgh, then all 3 Eastburgh players get + {{ gameConstants.LOCAL_REWARD }} coins. (You are NOT in Eastburgh and will NOT receive any coins from this bonus).
  4. If you choose to keep your coin, then you get + 1 coins.
