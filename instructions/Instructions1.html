Welcome to the experiment and thank you for joining. The next few pages will give you instructions on how to play.

  • You will play in a group of 6 players.
  • Each round, all 6 players are asked to make a decision.
  • You will play up to 25 rounds with the same players.
  • Each round, you can earn a bonus payment.
  • Your bonus payments depend on the decisions of the players in your group.

During the game you will occasionally have to wait for other members of your group.

You must stay active in the game. When it comes to a decision, you will have 30 seconds. If you miss decisions in 3 rounds then you will lose your bonus payments.
